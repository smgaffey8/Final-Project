Ethereal

Ethereal is an online recruiting platform for both prospective student athletes and college soccer scouts alike. Prospective students have the opportunity to display both their academic achievments as well as upload video highlights to show what they're capeable of on the field. Coaches and scouts that have been verified by the NCAA will then be able to search our network to find players that fit their specific academic requirements.
